📊 Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

Retail Sales Performance Dashboard created using Excel with Pivot Tables, Charts, and Slicers.

📌 **Project Overview**

      This project showcases a Retail Sales Performance Dashboard built using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard provides insights into sales trends, regional               performance, product demand, and category-level sales.

      It enables business users to quickly analyze sales performance and make data-driven decisions.

🎯 **Business Problem**

      Retail companies need to monitor sales performance across regions, months, and product categories to identify growth opportunities and improve sales               strategies.

      This dashboard helps answer key business questions such as:

      Which region generates the most revenue?
      Which product categories perform best?
      What are the monthly sales trends?
      Which products drive the highest sales?
      
📊 **Dashboard KPIs**

📈 **Dashboard Features**

    1️⃣ Monthly Sales Trend

        A line chart showing how sales fluctuate across months.

    2️⃣ Sales by Region

        A column chart comparing sales across different regions.

    3️⃣ Sales by Category

        A horizontal bar chart highlighting category-level sales performance.

    4️⃣ Top Performing Products

        A bar chart displaying the best-selling products.

🎛 **Interactive Filters**

    The dashboard includes slicers that allow dynamic filtering by:

    Region
    Category
    Month

This makes the dashboard interactive and user-friendly.

🔍 **Key Insights**

    Sales are highest in April and October, and lowest in May and August.
    The South region contributes the highest sales, while West region has the lowest sales.
    Electronics is the highest-selling category.
    T-Shirt is the best-performing product, followed by Mobile and Rice.
